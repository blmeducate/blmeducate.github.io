BlackLivesMatter

This site offers action items that address the policies and government officials
that allow police brutality to go unchecked.

  1. E-Mail: Send a pre-filled email to state representatives and senators demanding for legislation to address the criminal justice system and policing methods that perpetuate racism.

  2. Petitions: Links to petitions that advocate policy reform, demand justice for victims of police brutality, and call for the removal of racist government officials.

  3. Donate: Links to funds that support Black Lives Matter protestors, families of victims to police brutality, and organizations working towards the end of systemic racism.

  4. Policies: Information about the policies that enable cops to commit acts of police brutality without consequences.

We must change the system to make cops accountable for their actions!
